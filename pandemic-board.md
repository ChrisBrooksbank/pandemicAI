# Pandemic — Board & City Connections Reference

## Overview

The Pandemic board features **48 cities** connected by a network of routes. Cities are divided into 4 disease color regions with **12 cities each**. The board wraps horizontally — several connections cross the Pacific Ocean between the left and right edges of the map.

## Regional Notes

- **Atlanta** is the starting city for all players and begins with a research station.
- **Blue region** covers North America and Western/Northern Europe.
- **Yellow region** covers Latin America and Sub-Saharan Africa.
- **Black region** covers North Africa, the Middle East, and South/Central Asia.
- **Red region** covers East Asia, Southeast Asia, and Oceania.
- The **Pacific connections** (San Francisco–Tokyo, San Francisco–Manila, Los Angeles–Sydney) are strategically important for moving between the Americas and Asia.
- **Santiago** is the most isolated city on the board with only a single connection to Lima.
